Smart Building Energy Monitor — Product Requirements

Overview
Deploy sensors across commercial buildings to track HVAC and electrical usage in real time.

Devices
- Temperature and humidity sensors (per zone)
- Smart electricity meters on main panels
- Occupancy sensors in meeting rooms
- BACnet gateway for legacy HVAC controllers

Dashboard Requirements

KPIs
- Current total power draw (kW)
- Average zone temperature
- Daily energy cost estimate
- Occupancy rate (%)

Charts
- 24-hour line chart of temperature per zone
- Weekly bar chart comparing energy use by floor
- Trend area chart for humidity levels

Alerts
- Email and in-app alert when temperature exceeds 28°C for 15 minutes
- Critical alarm when meter reading spikes 20% above baseline
- Warning banner for offline sensors

Tables
- Sortable table of all devices with status, last seen, and firmware version
- Event log table for alert history (last 7 days)

Controls
- Toggle HVAC eco mode per zone
- Slider to set temperature setpoint (18–26°C)
- Button to acknowledge all active alerts
